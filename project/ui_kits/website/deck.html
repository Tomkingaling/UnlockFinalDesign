SEIS income tax relief is 50% of the amount invested, up to £200,000 per tax year. Loss relief is shown at 45% of net cost (additional rate) — a net loss of about 27.5p in the £ if it fails. Above the SEIS limits the founding round uses EIS: EIS income tax relief is 30% of the amount invested, up to £1,000,000 per tax year (£2,000,000 for Knowledge Intensive Companies).
