5 · Back matter — glossary & references
UNLOCK.Appendix

Glossary

BPR — Business Property Relief
Relief reducing IHT on qualifying business assets; capped at £2.5M per estate (Finance Act 2026).
EIS — Enterprise Investment Scheme
Tax-advantaged scheme: 30% income-tax relief; IHT-free after 2 years.
NRB — Nil-Rate Band
The £325,000 threshold below which no IHT is charged.
