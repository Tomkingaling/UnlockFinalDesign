Direction

  • Candid, in-context professionals (40s–70s) — investors, advisers, families.
  • Warm neutral backgrounds; soft natural light; eye contact or considered gaze.
  • Masked into the unlock shapes; one Jade block per composition, never more.
  • Greyscale + Jade duotone for covers / heroes; full-colour for content rows.
  • No glossy stock, no abstract 3D, no busy backgrounds.
